<p><strong>The Clinical Mass Spectrometry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LC-MS-MS</li><li>MALDI-TOF</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The clinical mass spectrometry market is primarily categorized into three types: LC-MS/MS, MALDI-TOF, and others. LC-MS/MS (Liquid Chromatography-Tandem Mass Spectrometry) is widely used for its sensitivity and specificity in analyzing complex biological samples. MALDI-TOF (Matrix-Assisted Laser Desorption/Ionization-Time of Flight) is favored for rapid microbial identification and biomolecule analysis. The "others" category encompasses various emerging techniques and innovations that enhance the capabilities of mass spectrometry in clinical diagnostics, research, and drug development.</p></p>
