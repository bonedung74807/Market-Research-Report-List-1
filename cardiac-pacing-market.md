<p><strong>The Cardiac Pacing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Temporary Cardiac Pacing</li><li>Single-Chamber Cardiac Pacing</li><li>Dual-Chamber Cardiac Pacing</li><li>Single-Chamber ICD</li><li>Dual-Chamber ICD</li><li>Three- Chamber CRT-P</li><li>Three- Chamber CRT-D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac pacing market consists of various device types tailored for different patient needs. Temporary cardiac pacing provides short-term support, while single-chamber pacing targets one heart chamber for basic rhythm management. Dual-chamber pacing coordinates both atrial and ventricular activity. Implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICDs), including single and dual-chamber models, are designed for arrhythmia prevention. Three-chamber devices, like CRT-P and CRT-D, offer advanced therapies for heart failure by synchronizing contractions across the heart's ventricles, improving overall cardiac function.</p></p>
