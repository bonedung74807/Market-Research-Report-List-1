<p><p>Soy protein concentrates are produced through various processes, each yielding distinct market types. The Aqueous Alcohol Washing Process removes soluble carbohydrates, enhancing protein content while preserving functionality. Acid Washing Process utilizes acids to coagulate proteins, producing a concentrate with specific functional properties, ideal for food applications. The Heat Denaturation Process involves applying heat to alter protein structure, improving solubility and digestibility. Each type caters to diverse industries such as food, beverages, and nutritional supplements, addressing varying consumer demands and preferences.</p></p>
