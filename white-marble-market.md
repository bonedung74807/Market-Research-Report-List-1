<p><p>The white marble market can be categorized into two main types: natural marble and artificial marble. Natural marble is a high-quality, naturally occurring stone prized for its unique patterns and durability, commonly used in premium construction and design projects. Artificial marble, on the other hand, is a man-made alternative, often composed of resins and minerals, designed to mimic the appearance of natural stone while offering versatility and affordability. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences, influencing market dynamics significantly.</p></p>
