Composite synthetic marble combines different materials for enhanced strength and aesthetic appeal. Sintered synthetic marble is created through high-temperature processes, providing a dense and non-porous surface, ideal for heavy-use environments. Each type serves distinct preferences in design, functionality, and application.</p></p>
